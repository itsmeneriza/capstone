Facility Usage Policy

By submitting a request, you agree to the following terms and conditions:

General Policies

  • • Priority: Students and school activities, as well as government activities, have priority in facility usage.
  • • Responsibility: The user is responsible for ensuring the facility is clean and in proper condition after use.
  • • Equipment: Any damage to school property will be billed to the applicant at replacement cost.
  • • Schedule: Time extensions must be requested at least 24 hours in advance and are subject to availability.
  • • Food & Drinks: Strictly prohibited in the Auditorium and Library. Other facilities may allow with proper cleanup.
  • • Requirements: Valid ID and complete contact information must be provided for all reservations.

Payment & Fees

  • Reservation Fee: A non-refundable down payment of ₱2,000.00 is required to secure your reservation.
  • Payment Deadline: Final settlement must be completed at least 3 working days before the event. Unpaid reservations risk immediate cancellation if priority activities arise.
  • • Payment Location: All payments must be made at the Administration Office during office hours (8:00 AM - 5:00 PM, Monday to Friday).

Cancellation Policy

  • • Notice Suggestion: We recommend providing at least 2 weeks notice for any cancellation to allow proper rescheduling.
  • Restriction: Cancellations will NOT be accepted within 2 weeks of the planned event date. The down payment is non-refundable under all circumstances.

Equipment & Parking

  • • Sound Systems: Not provided by the school. Renters must arrange their own audio equipment if needed.
  • • Additional Equipment: Tables, chairs, and other equipment may be available upon request with additional fees.
  • • Parking: Parking facilities are managed by the city government and are not included in the rental fee. Separate arrangements must be made.
I have read and understood all BCHS facility guidelines, including general policies, payment requirements, cancellation restrictions, and equipment/parking information.
